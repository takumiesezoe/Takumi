I get dressed in sounds

Called music

It's a beat, a pulse, louder than my own.

It breaks me,

it heals me,

it drowns me,

it saves me.

  
I vanish in echoes,

and what remains

is rhythm wearing my skin.

  

It is color, it is movement, it is nature, it is life
